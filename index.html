Hi! I’m Qingwei Ben (贲清炜), second-year Ph.D. of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong supervised by Prof. Dahua Lin. My research focuses on Embodied AI, especially Humanoid Loco-Manipulation. I am now working with Dr. Jiangmiao Pang and Dr. Jingbo Wang at Intern Robotics, Shanghai AI Lab. Previously, I obtained my bachelor degree at Weiyang College, Tsinghua University, majoring in Mathematics and Physics + Software Engineering. During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to work under the supervision of Prof. Huazhe Xu at the TEA Lab, IIIS, Tsinghua University and Prof. Chuang Gan at UMASS Amherst. If you are interested in having a discussion with me, please drop me an e-mail at elgceben@gmail.com or my wechat: elgceben.
